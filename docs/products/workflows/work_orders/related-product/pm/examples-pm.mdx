## Work Order Creation {#create-wo}

<div className="alert alert--primary">



With the _Preventive Maintenance_ product, work orders are automatically created and sent to stakeholders when time-based or value-based triggers are set off.


<img alt="create work order" className="img_sizing item shadow--tl" src={useBaseUrl('img/products/video_create_work_order(pm).gif')} />
<br/>

Once submitted, the _Work Order_ workflow is initiated, and the work order is sent to contractors.

## Accept Work Order {#accept}

The person assigned with doing the maintenance check has to fill out the **Accept Work Order** form. If accepted, they will recieve a **Checklist** form where they indicate the maintenance checks performmed on the vehicle.

## Close Work Order {#close-wo}

Once the task is complete, the **Close Work Order** form must be filled out. To finally close the workflow, the supervisor must approve the job and send any corresponding feedback.
