<span className="hero__title">Survey Forms</span>
<br/>
<br/>

Rate, write, and send multimedia files with the feedback form that appears in the channel workspace after submitting an approved [_Work Order Reception_](/docs/products/workflows/work_orders/related-product/pm/surveys-acceptance) form.


<div className="alert alert--primary">

_To access the form:_

<img alt="feedback" className="img_sizing item shadow--tl" src={useBaseUrl('img/products/product_preventive_wo_feedback_00.png')} />
<br/>

1. Select <span className="badge badge--primary">Work orders</span> on the **Main Menu Bar**.
2. From the **Work orders** panel, choose the corresponding work order in the _Closed_ state.
3. Find the **Feedback** form on the channel workspace.


**NOTE:** All stakeholders will receive a copy of the submitted _Feedback_ form.
