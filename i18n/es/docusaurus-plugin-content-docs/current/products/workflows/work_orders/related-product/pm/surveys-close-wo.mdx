## Close Work Order {#close}

After completing the maintenance job, contractors can request to close the work order by filling out the corresponding form.

<div className="alert alert--primary">

To open the form:

<img alt="close work order form" className="img_sizing item shadow--tl" src={useBaseUrl('img/products/product_preventive_wo_close_00.png')} />
<br/>

1. On the **Main Menu Bar**, press <span className="badge badge--primary">Work orders</span>.
2. Choose the corresponding work order from the **Work orders** panel.
3. Press the green actions button at the bottom of the channel workspace.
4. Select **Close work order** from the menu. The form will open up as shown below.

</div>
<br/>

<div className="alert alert--primary">

_The **Close Work Order** form opens up in the channel workspace as shown below:_

<img alt="close work order form" className="img_sizing item shadow--tl" src={useBaseUrl('img/products/product_preventive_wo_close_01.png')} />
<br/>

</div>
<br/>

<div className="alert alert--primary">

_The form is connected to your master data, allowing you to add the cost of services and discount from stock the materials used in the job._

<img alt="close work order form" className="img_sizing_small item shadow--tl" src={useBaseUrl('img/products/product_preventive_wo_close_02.png')} />
<br/>

</div>

:::info Next Step
After the **Close Work Order** form is sent, supervisors must [approve the maintenance job](/docs/products/workflows/work_orders/related-product/pm/surveys-acceptance), thus officially closing the work order.
:::
